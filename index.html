Cleaning up the details that decide whether a store feels trusted

Most Shopify requests are not one big rebuild. They are a stack of details that affect trust: product sections that break on mobile, spacing that feels uneven, a theme setting that cannot quite match the brand, or a launch page that needs final cleanup. For client work, I usually start by finding the friction in the buying path, then adjust Liquid, CSS, theme settings, and responsive behavior without disturbing the store's existing content or apps. The outcome is a storefront that feels cleaner, easier to manage, and more prepared for real customers.
